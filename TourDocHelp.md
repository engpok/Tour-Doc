# Tour Doc — Help

Tour Doc lets you **record a walking tour** with a breadcrumb trail, segment markers, photos, and text notes.

---

## Record a Tour

1. Go to **Record**
2. Tap **Start**
3. Walk — the app records a breadcrumb trail on the map
4. Tap **Mark** whenever you want to create a new stop  
   - Mark also opens the camera so you can take a photo right away
5. Tap **Photo** to add a photo to the most recent segment
6. Tap **Pause** to stop GPS and save battery  
   Tap **Resume** to continue
7. Tap **Stop** to save the tour

---

## Add captions

- Under each photo, tap the text box to type a caption.
- Captions are saved automatically.

---

## View and edit saved tours

1. Go to **Tours**
2. Tap a tour to open it
3. Edit captions directly in the list
4. Swipe a photo for:
   - **Delete**
   - **Copy to Photos**
5. Swipe a tour in the list to **Rename** or **Delete**

---

## Export

Open a saved tour and tap **Export**:

- **PDF** — best for sharing/printing
- **DOCX** — editable in Word (Mac/Windows)

Exports include:
- A map with numbered pins
- A segment list
- Photos and captions

---

## Settings

- **Units:** miles or kilometers
- **Default export format:** PDF or DOCX

---

## Tips

- If a segment shows “Location unavailable,” GPS accuracy was low or the last fix was too old.
- For best results, allow location access and keep the app active while recording.
