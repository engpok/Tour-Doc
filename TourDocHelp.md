# Tour Doc — Help

Tour Doc lets you:
 - **Record a walking tour** with a breadcrumb trail, photos, and text notes.
   - You **mark** places where you stop to take photos and/or add notes
 - **View a saved tour**:
   - Pins on the map show you where you stopped to take photos or add notes
   - Get directions to Pinned locations (paid version only)
   - Export photos to Photos app
 - **Export the saved tour to PDF**
 - **Export the tour to Word format for editing** (paid version only)

---
# Terminology
 - Mark: a command to "mark" this location on the map and assign the next photos and notes to this location
 - Segment: a set of photos and notes associated with a marked location

## Record a Tour

1. Go to **Record** tab
2. Change default **tour name** if desired
3. Tap **Start**
4. Walk — the app records a breadcrumb trail on the map
5. Tap **Mark** whenever you want to pin a new location of the map  
   - Mark also opens the camera so you can take a photo right away
6. Tap **Photo** to add a photo to the most recent segment
7. Tap **Pause** to stop GPS and save battery  
   Tap **Resume** to continue
8. Tap **Stop** to save the tour

---

## Add captions

- Under each photo, tap the text box to type a caption.
- Captions are saved automatically.

---

## View and edit saved tours

1. Go to **Tours** tab
2. Tap a tour to open it
3. Edit captions directly in the list
4. Swipe a photo for:
   - **Delete**
   - **Copy to Photos**
5. Swipe a location/segment header to get direction to that location (paid version only)
6. Swipe a tour in the list of tours to **Rename** or **Delete**

---

## Export

Open a saved tour and tap **Export**:

- **PDF** — best for sharing/printing
- **DOCX** — editable in Word (Mac/Windows) (paid version only)

Exports include:
- A map with numbered pins
- A segment list
- Photos and captions

---

## Settings

- **Units:** miles or kilometers
- **Default export format:** PDF or DOCX

---

## Tips

- If a segment shows “Location unavailable,” GPS accuracy was low or the last fix was too old.
- For best results, allow location access and keep the app active while recording.
